# BErozgar Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 21, 2026
Last Updated: February 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how BErozgar collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data.

## 1. Data We Collect

We may collect:
- Account data: name, email, role, verification status.
- Authentication data: password hash (if password auth), Google identity metadata.
- Session/security data: refresh token hashes, CSRF/security metadata, IP/user-agent logs.
- Marketplace data: listings, requests, dispute data, timestamps.
- Operational data: audit logs, rate-limit events, fraud/risk indicators.

We do not store plaintext passwords.

## 2. How We Use Data

We use data to:
- Authenticate users and protect accounts.
- Operate listing/request/dispute workflows.
- Enforce trust, safety, and moderation policies.
- Detect abuse and investigate incidents.
- Maintain service reliability and auditing.

## 3. Legal Basis and Consent Context

For a campus platform context, processing is based on:
- User action to access platform services.
- Legitimate operational and security interests.
- Explicit user submissions within the app.

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data.

Data may be shared only with:
- Authorized admin/moderation personnel.
- Service providers needed for hosting, authentication, and infrastructure.
- Authorities when legally required.

## 5. Data Security

We implement technical controls including:
- Access control and role checks.
- Token/session hardening.
- Logging and audit trail mechanisms.
- Rate limits and anti-abuse protections.

No internet system is perfectly secure; users should also maintain account hygiene.

## 6. Cookies and Sessions

BErozgar uses secure session mechanisms, including httpOnly cookies for refresh/session flows where configured.

## 7. Data Retention

Retention windows and deletion principles are defined in:
- `docs/legal/DATA_RETENTION_POLICY.md`

## 8. User Rights and Requests

Users may request correction or deletion of account-linked data, subject to:
- Ongoing dispute/security/legal obligations.
- Audit and integrity requirements.

## 9. Minors

BErozgar is intended for college users. If local policy requires guardian consent for specific users, access may be restricted accordingly.

## 10. Policy Updates

We may revise this policy. Updates are indicated by the Last Updated date.

## 11. Contact

For privacy requests, contact the BErozgar admin team through official project channels.
